Crash Bandicoot: The Wrath of Cortex
(PS3)
In a newly-built space station orbiting the Earth, Uka Uka, having called a convention of recurring villains in the series, berates Dr. Cortex, Tiny Tiger, Dr. N. Tropy, Dr. N. Gin and Dingodile for their recently unimpressive evil productivity, and forces them to come up with a plan to eliminate Crash Bandicoot, their main opponent.
