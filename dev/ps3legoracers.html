Lego Racers
(Playstation 1)
Rocket Racer and Veronica Voltage are traveling through time and space in search of the best racers in the galaxy. These racers are Captain Redbeard, King Kahuka, Basil the Bat Lord, Johnny Thunder, Baron von Barron, and Gypsy Moth, all of whom host their own racing circuits. In an attempt to become champion, the player takes them and fellow racers on. Once all of them are defeated, Rocket Racer is challenged. The credits roll when he is defeated in a race.
